About Us

Hi there! And thank you for visiting our site. Here you can learn a little more about FUBR!

Operated by a Proud U.S Army Veteran

Hi, I’m Lance — owner and operator of FUBR! A small home workshop

By trade, I’m a software developer, but I’ve always had a deep passion for CNC machining, CAD design, and 3D scanning. I started making custom foam inserts back in my Army days (1998–2008), originally for myself, friends, and family. Like many of us in the military, I had a healthy collection of firearms — and wanted a way to organize and showcase them without compromising on utility or style. What I didn’t like were the cheap, standard foam inserts that came with hard cases. So, I decided to machine my own. The results? Sleeker designs, better fitment, and smart layouts that maximize every inch of case space. And they just look cooler. At FUBR, I take pride in making inserts that are custom, clean, and crafted with care.
